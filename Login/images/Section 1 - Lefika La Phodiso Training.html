Section 1: Introducing the module

Welcome to module 2 of community art counselling.

When completing this module, the learner will demonstrate the ability to:

  • Identify common issues/difficulties in the therapeutic process of counselling. 
  • Determine a counselling approach to match group members’ needs.
  • Establish an appropriate context for counselling. 

You will need approximately 30 hours to complete the full module

Module 2 has 14 sections and several different types of activities, including videos, transcripts of the videos, group activities, individual activities, quizzes, optional assignments and a final assessment. Activities and options assignments must be completed in either your journal, the creation of an image, or a Word document. Journal entries and images must be photographed and uploaded to your student dashboard.  You will be directed to additional readings and videos where appropriate.

In this module, we will introduce the counselling process by: 

  • defining a person-centred approach to counselling
  • demonstrating effective counselling skills

We will also unpack counselling by looking at: 

  • ethics
  • characteristics of a counsellor
  • counselling propositions
  • sympathy/empathy
  • endings

We will also look at different group activities by exploring and practising counselling skills, active listening, identifying emotions, amongst others.

Art materials

No new or additional art materials are needed for this module.
